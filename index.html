There Are Eight Planets in Our Solar System

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

Mercury

The planet mercury has no moons. Mercury is the second hottest planet, but there is a dark side of
the planet that has rain. Unlike Earth a year on Mercury is only 88 days long!!!

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. Venus is sometimes referred to as Earths sister planet
becuase of it's similar size and mass. Venus is also named after the Roman God of love and beauty.
A year on venus is 255 earth days and a day on Venus would be 177 days on Earth. Also Venus is
has no rings and no moons.

Earth (our planet)

Earth is the only planet that has been known to support life. It is the largest planet in our solar system
and also has a magnetic field. Unlike other planets Earth is not named after a god. It takes 365 days for
Earth to rotate around the sun. It used to be thought that Earth was the center of the universe.
