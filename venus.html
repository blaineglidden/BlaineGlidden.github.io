Venus

Venus is the second planet from the sun and is the closest planet to Earth. Venus is the hottest planet with a surface temperature of 867℉(464℃). The atmosphere on venus extends about 50 miles and is carbon dioxide, out of all the rocky planets Venus has the thickest and densest atmosphere. About 20%of the sunlight reaches Venus’s surface and the other 80% if off of the planets clouds. The atmosphere hides volcanos and plains of lava. The planets is named after the Roman goddess of love and all of the surfaces on Venus are named after women, except one. The Maat Mons (named after an Egyptian goddess) is the tallest volcano on Venus, rising 5miles and the lava flows extend for hundreds of miles. The Venera craft was the first craft to land on Venus’s surface. Venera 9 sent back the first image of the planets surface in black and white in 1975 and Venera 13 sent back the first color image of the planets surface in 1982.
